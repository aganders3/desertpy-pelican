About DesertPy

DesertPy is a group of Pythonistas in the Phoenix, Arizona area.

  • DesertPy Meetup Group
  • @desertpy on twitter

Meetings

Our group meets at 6:30PM on the fourth Wednesday of every other month (February, April, June, August, October, December). Our current meeting place has been Gangplank Chandler in downtown Chandler, AZ.

On our off months we occasionally get together for a social meeting elsewhere.

Contributing

  • Come to meetings

  • Give a presentation at a meeting
  • Write a blog post on our website
